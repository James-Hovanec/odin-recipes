Taco Bowl

The most delicious ground beef with taco seasoning over a bed of white rice and topped with avocado, corn, tomatoes with cheese and sour cream. Perfect for taco Tuesday!

Ingredients List

  • 1 pound taco meat
  • 1/2 cup of white rice
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 Tbsp cilantro
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 1 avocado
  • 1/4 red onion
  • 1 cup of tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Hot sauce or sriracha
  • 1 lime

Steps to make!

  1. Prepare Taco Meat
  2. Cook white rice. Drain and drizzle with lime juice
  3. In a skillet, saute corn with 1 Tbsp of butter
  4. Prepare all the desired topping from the list above. Slice the onions, cut the avocados and cube the tomatoes
  5. In a bowl, arrange all the ingredients for this taco bowl
  6. Enjoy.
